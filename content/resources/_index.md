In this section we describe the key resources you can configure for your **Organization** — whether running on the Cloud edition at [snapcd.io](https://snapcd.io) or on a [self-hosted]({{< relref "components/server" >}}) Server. Each resource corresponds to a [Terraform Provider](https://registry.terraform.io/providers/schrieksoft/snapcd/latest/docs) resource type.
